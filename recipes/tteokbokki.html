Tteokbokki

Description

Tteokbokki is Korean dish made of spicy rice cakes. It is a popular street food.

Ingredients

  • 350g Korean rice cakes
  • 150g Korean fish cakes
  • 2 cups dashi stock or 2 cups water*
  • 1 hard-boiled egg
  • 1 stalk green onion, sliced
  • *if using water, 1 tsp hondashi
  • 3 Tbsp gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1.5 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp minced garlic

Steps

  1. Soak the rice cakes in warm water for 10 minutes.
  2. Rinse the fish cakes.
  3. In a pot, mix the gochujang, sugar, soy sauce, and minced garlic with the stock or water and hondashi.
  4. Heat the sauce mixture over medium high heat. Once boiling, add the drained rice cakes and fish cakes.
  5. Simmer until the rice cakes are soft and fully cooked, and until the sauce is thickened.
  6. Garnish with green onion and serve with a hard-boiled egg sliced in half.
