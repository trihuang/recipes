Gimbap

Description

Gimbap is a Korean rice roll wrapped in seaweed and filled with ingredients such as tuna, ham, egg, and vegetables. There are many varieties of gimbap. You can use any type of filling.

Ingredients (for 1 roll)

  • 1 dried seaweed sheet
  • steamed rice
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/4 carrot, julienned
  • 1 imitation crab stick
  • 1 strip of yellow pickled radish
  • kimchi
  • sesame oil
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat a pan. Add a bit of oil and spread it around the pan. Add the beaten egg and cook both sides of the omelette.
  2. Saute the carrots.
  3. Cook the imitation crab stick in the pan.
  4. Cut the omelette into wide strips.
  5. Add a dash of sesame oil and a pinch of salt (seasoned to taste) to a bowl of steamed rice.
  6. Place a sheet of seaweed on a bamboo rolling mat. Spread rice thinly to cover 2/3 of the seaweed.
  7. Place the filling ingredients in the center.
  8. Roll the seaweed.
  9. Brush some sesame oil on the surface of the gimbap.
  10. Slice the gimbap into bite-sized pieces.
  11. Enjoy!
